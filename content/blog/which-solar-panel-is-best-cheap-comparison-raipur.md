  <p>If you have a terrace with white tiles or light paint, <strong>Bifacial panels</strong> are a game-changer. They absorb sunlight from the front and reflected light from the back.   <h2 id="heat-impact">3. The Temperature Coefficient: The Silent Profit Killer</h2>
  <p>Every solar panel has a "Temperature Coefficient." It tells you how much power is lost for every degree above 25°C. Raipur often hits 45°C. A panel with a poor coefficient will lose 15-20% of its rated capacity just because it's hot. At MDNetwork, we mandate a coefficient of <strong>at least -0.35%/°C</strong> for all our Raipur installations.</p>

  <h2 id="maintenance">4. Maintenance Reality: Dust & Pollution</h2>
  <p>Raipur's industrial proximity means dust accumulation is high. If you don't clean your panels every 15 days, you lose 10% efficiency. We recommend all our residential clients in Samta Colony and GE Road install a simple rooftop water spray system.</p>
