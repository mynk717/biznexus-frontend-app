  <h2 id="bifacial">2. The Bifacial Advantage</h2>
  <p>If you have a terrace with white or light-colored tiles, <strong>Bifacial panels</strong> are a game-changer. They absorb sunlight from both the front and the back (reflecting off your roof). In Raipur, this can increase your generation by another 10-15%, making your ROI period significantly shorter. For a detailed cost breakdown of a full system using these panels, see our <a href="/blog/3-kilowatt-solar-panel-price-raipur-2026" class="text-blue-600 underline font-bold">3kW solar price master guide</a>.</p>

  <h2 id="heat-impact">3. Why Temperature Coefficient Matters</h2>
  <p>Every solar panel has a "Temperature Coefficient." It tells you how much power is lost for every degree above 25°C. Raipur often hits 45°C. Mono-PERC panels have a much better coefficient than older Polycrystalline ones, meaning they lose significantly less power during our scorching afternoons.</p>
