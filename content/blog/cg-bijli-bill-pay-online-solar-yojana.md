  <h2 id="check-bill">1. How to Check Your CG Bijli Bill Online (2026)</h2>
  <p>To check your current outstanding amount from CSPDCL, you need your 10-digit <strong>Consumer Number (BP Number)</strong> found at the top of your physical bill. You can view your bill through:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>CSPDCL Official Website:</strong> Visit the <a href="https://www.cspdcl.co.in" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="text-blue-600 underline">CSPDCL Web Self Service</a> portal.</li>
    <li><strong>Mor Bijli App:</strong> The official Chhattisgarh state mobile app for real-time bill tracking.</li>
    <li><strong>Digital Wallets:</strong> Apps like PhonePe, GPay, and Paytm also allow instant "CG Bijli Bill" retrieval using your consumer ID.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 id="yojana-pivot">3. From Bill Payer to Energy Producer: The Solar Panel Yojana</h2>
  <p>The <strong>Solar Panel Yojana</strong> (officially known as PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana) is specifically designed for people with active CSPDCL connections. If you have a valid consumer number and a shadow-free roof, you are eligible for the highest subsidies in India.</p>
  
  <div class="p-6 bg-blue-50 dark:bg-blue-900/20 border-l-4 border-blue-500 rounded-r-xl my-10 not-prose">
    <p class="text-sm text-slate-900 dark:text-slate-100 font-bold mb-2">The "Consumer Number" Link:</p>
    <p class="text-slate-700 dark:text-slate-300">You cannot apply for the solar subsidy without your CG Bijli Bill. The PM Surya Ghar portal uses your consumer number to verify your 12-month average consumption and determine if your local transformer can handle a solar installation.</p>
