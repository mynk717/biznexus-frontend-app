  <h2 id="check-bill">1. How to Check Your CG Bijli Bill Online (2026)</h2>
  <p>To access your account on the <strong>CSPDCL (Chhattisgarh State Power Distribution Company Ltd)</strong> portal, you must first locate your 10-digit **Consumer Number**. This is often labeled as 'BP Number' or 'Service Number' on the top left of your physical bill.</p>
  
  <h3 id="payment-portals">Official Payment Channels:</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>CSPDCL Web Portal:</strong> The most reliable method for downloading high-resolution PDF bills. Go to the 'Web Self Service' section.</li>
    <li><strong>Mor Bijlee App:</strong> The state's official app which now supports **Photo Spot Billing**—providing visual proof of your meter reading to eliminate "human error" complaints.</li>
    <li><strong>Third-Party Apps:</strong> While PhonePe and Google Pay are convenient for quick payments, they **do not** provide the detailed breakup of fixed charges and state subsidies that you need for solar ROI calculations.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 id="download-bill">2. How to Download a Duplicate CG Bijli Bill</h2>
  <p>Many homeowners in neighborhoods like **Shankar Nagar and Telibandha** need duplicate bills for KYC or bank loan applications. Here is the insider secret to getting yours:</p>
  <ol>
    <li>Log in to the CSPDCL official portal.</li>
    <li>Navigate to **'Bill Information'** → **'View/Print Bill'**.</li>
    <li>Select your billing month. You can access up to **24 months of payment history**, which is essential when auditing your home's energy profile for a new solar installation.</li>
  </ol>

  <h2 id="mor-bijlee">3. Advanced Features of the Mor Bijlee App</h2>
  <p>Chhattisgarh's **Mor Bijlee** app is one of the most advanced utility apps in India. Here are three features that most users miss:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Service Line Self-Verification:</strong> You can take photos of your service cable and materials and upload them for remote verification by area officers. This is a massive time-saver if you are applying for a **Load Change**.</li>
    <li><strong>Real-time Smart Meter Monitoring:</strong> If your Raipur home has a Smart Meter, the app shows your "Relay Status"—letting you know if your power was cut remotely or due to a local fault.</li>
    <li><strong>Urgent Accident Reporting:</strong> A dedicated portal for dangerous electrical situations that bypasses the standard complaint queue.</li>
  </ul>
