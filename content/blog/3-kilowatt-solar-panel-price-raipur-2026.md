  <h3 id="paa-subsidy">People Also Ask: Common 3kW Subsidy Questions</h3>
  
  <h4 class="font-bold mt-6 mb-2">How much is the subsidy for a 3kW solar system in 2026?</h4>
  <p class="text-sm mb-4">Under the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana, the subsidy for a 3kW system is exactly **₹78,000**. This is calculated as ₹30,000/kW for the first 2kW and ₹18,000 for the 3rd kW. Any capacity beyond 3kW does not receive additional central subsidy.</p>

  <h4 class="font-bold mt-6 mb-2">Does the subsidy apply to off-grid 3kW systems?</h4>
  <p class="text-sm mb-4">No. The national subsidy scheme is strictly for **On-Grid (Grid-Tied)** systems that help reduce the load on the national grid. Off-grid systems with batteries are not eligible for the PM Surya Ghar incentives.</p>

  <h4 class="font-bold mt-6 mb-2">How long does it take to receive the solar subsidy in Raipur?</h4>
  <p class="text-sm mb-4">Once the system is commissioned and the net meter is installed by CSPDCL, the subsidy is typically credited to your linked bank account within **30 to 45 days** of the final inspection report upload.</p>

  <h3 id="how-to-apply">How to Apply for 3kW Solar Subsidy in Raipur</h3>
  <p>To ensure you get your ₹78,000 back from the government, follow these specific steps on the National Portal. This is the only way to achieve the best ROI:</p>
  <div class="p-6 bg-slate-100 rounded-2xl my-8">
    <ol class="space-y-4">
      <li><strong>Step 1: Portal Registration</strong> – Use your CSPDCL consumer number and mobile to register on the PM Surya Ghar portal at <a href="https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">pmsuryaghar.gov.in</a>.</li>
      <li><strong>Step 2: Feasibility Approval</strong> – CSPDCL officials will check if your local transformer can handle the 3kW export. This usually takes 7-10 days in Raipur.</li>
      <li><strong>Step 3: Installation</strong> – You **must** use an empanelled vendor. Using a non-registered installer will disqualify you from the subsidy.</li>
      <li><strong>Step 4: Net Metering</strong> – After installation, a bidirectional meter (Net Meter) is installed by CSPDCL to track your export to the grid.</li>
      <li><strong>Step 5: Subsidy Release</strong> – Once the commissioning report is uploaded, the subsidy is credited directly to your bank account within 30 days.</li>
    </ol>
  </div>

  <h2 id="cost-breakdown">2. Detailed 3kW Cost Breakdown: Components & Labor</h2>
  <p>When you see a quote for the total cost, it's vital to look beyond the total figure. A solar system is only as strong as its weakest link. In Raipur's extreme weather, using "low-cost" wiring or standard galvanized structures can lead to system failure within 5 years. Here is the technical breakdown of what constitutes a robust 3kW investment.</p>

  <h3 id="panel-costs">A. The Solar Panels (60% of Cost)</h3>
  <p>The bulk of your investment goes into the modules. For a 3kW system, you typically need 6 to 10 panels depending on the wattage (e.g., 540Wp vs 330Wp). We recommend using **Mono-PERC Half-Cut** cells for Raipur. These panels have a better temperature coefficient, meaning they perform significantly better during Raipur's 45°C summers compared to traditional modules. For a technical breakdown of which modules perform best in our heat, see our <a href="/blog/which-solar-panel-is-best-cheap-comparison-raipur" class="text-blue-600 underline">mono-PERC vs bifacial panel comparison</a>.</p>

  <h3 id="inverter-costs">B. The Inverter (20% of Cost)</h3>
  <p>The inverter is the "brain" of your home. It converts the DC electricity from your roof into AC power for your appliances. For an on-grid system, the inverter must also sync perfectly with CSPDCL's grid frequency. If the grid goes down, the inverter must shut off immediately (anti-islanding) to protect line workers. A high-quality inverter from brands like Growatt or Goodwe costs more but offers superior Wi-Fi monitoring, allowing you to track daily generation from your phone while you're at work in Jaistambh Chowk.</p>

  <h3 id="structure-balance">C. Balance of System (BoS) & Labor (20% of Cost)</h3>
  <p>This includes the "unseen" heroes of your system:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Mounting Structures:</strong> We use hot-dip galvanized steel to prevent rust during Chhattisgarh's heavy monsoons.</li>
    <li><strong>DC/AC Cables:</strong> UV-protected, fire-retardant cables are mandatory to prevent fire hazards.</li>
    <li><strong>Liaisoning:</strong> This is the cost of managing the CSPDCL net-metering application, inspection, and final solar meter installation.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 id="inverter-types">4. 3 Kilowatt Solar Inverter – Types & Price Analysis</h2>
  
  <div class="quick-answer p-4 bg-slate-50 border border-slate-200 rounded-xl mb-6 not-prose">

  <p>Many homeowners ignore the inverter, focusing only on panels. This is a mistake. In Raipur, where voltage fluctuations are common, your inverter choice determines your system's uptime. There are three main types available in the Chhattisgarh market:</p>

  <h3 id="string-inverters">A. Standard String Inverters (Budget Friendly)</h3>
  <p>These are the most common in Raipur homes. All panels are connected in a single "string." If one panel is shaded by a tree in Samta Colony, the generation of the entire string drops. <strong>Price Range: ₹28,000 - ₹38,000.</strong></p>

  <h3 id="micro-inverters">B. Micro-Inverters (Maximum Efficiency)</h3>
  <p>Each panel has its own tiny inverter. This is the best solution for rooftops with partial shading issues. While it increases the upfront cost, it can increase your generation by 20% in shaded conditions. <strong>Price Range: ₹45,000 - ₹65,000.</strong></p>

  <h4 class="font-bold mt-6 mb-2">How much power does a 3kW solar system produce daily in Raipur?</h4>
  <p class="text-sm mb-4">On average, a 3kW system in Raipur generates **13 to 15 units (kWh) per day**. During peak summer months, this can go up to 18 units, while in monsoon it may drop to 8-10 units. Total annual generation is approx. 4,500 units.</p>

  <h2 id="on-grid-best">5. 3kW On-Grid Solar – The Best Choice for Raipur Residents</h2>
  <p>Unless you live in a very remote farmhouse, an **On-Grid system** is your best bet. Because of Raipur's reliable grid and CSPDCL's net-metering policy, it allows you to achieve a near-zero bill without the massive cost of batteries.</p>
  
  <div class="summary-box p-4 bg-slate-100 rounded-xl mb-10 not-prose">
    <p class="text-sm"><strong>Key Takeaway:</strong> On-grid solar uses the grid as a virtual battery. You export excess solar during the day and import it back at night for free. This is the only system eligible for the ₹78,000 subsidy.</p>
  </div>

  <h2 id="battery-price">6. 3kW Solar With Battery: Hybrid vs Off-Grid Options</h2>
  
  <h4 class="font-bold mt-6 mb-2">Is 3kW solar enough to run a 1.5-ton AC?</h4>
  <p class="text-sm mb-4">Yes. A 3kW system can comfortably run one **1.5-ton inverter AC** along with standard household loads like fans and lights during peak sunny hours. For running AC at night, you will either draw from the grid (On-grid) or from a battery bank (Hybrid).</p>

  <p>For those who want total independence, the costs are significantly higher:</p>

    <li><strong>Off-Grid System (Battery Only):</strong> ₹2.90L - ₹3.80L. Best for areas with frequent 4+ hour power cuts.</li>
    <li><strong>Hybrid System (Grid + Battery):</strong> ₹2.40L - ₹3.20L. The perfect "middle ground" for modern Raipur families.</li>
  </ul>
