  <h2 id="cspdcl-process">4. The Step-by-Step CSPDCL Registration Process</h2>
  <p>Ensuring your application is processed correctly by CSPDCL is vital for a smooth installation:</p>
  <ol>
    <li><strong>Registration:</strong> Register on the <a href="https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="text-blue-600 hover:underline">PM-Surya Ghar Portal</a> using your CSPDCL Consumer Number and Registered Mobile Number.</li>
    <li><strong>Technical Feasibility:</strong> CSPDCL will check your local transformer capacity. This usually takes 7-10 days in Raipur.</li>
    <li><strong>Vendor Selection:</strong> Choose an empanelled vendor. Note that installing through a non-empanelled vendor will disqualify you from the subsidy.</li>
    <li><strong>Net Metering Application:</strong> Once installed, your vendor applies for a Net Meter. CSPDCL will replace your existing meter with a Bi-directional meter.</li>
    <li><strong>Subsidy Credit:</strong> After the "Commissioning Certificate" is generated, the subsidy is credited to your bank account within 30 days.</li>
  </ol>
