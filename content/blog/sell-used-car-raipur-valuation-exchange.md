<h2 id="mistakes">5. 5 Mistakes Raipur Sellers Must Avoid</h2>
<div class="space-y-4 my-8">
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 1: Accepting Cash Without a Sale Deed</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm mt-2"><strong>What Happens:</strong> No proof of delivery. If the buyer disappears, the car remains your liability.</p>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm"><strong>Fix:</strong> Always sign a Delivery Note and keep a copy of the buyer's Aadhaar.</p>
</div>
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 2: Selling with an Active Loan</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm mt-2"><strong>What Happens:</strong> Transfer will be flatly rejected at Bhanpuri RTO.</p>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm"><strong>Fix:</strong> Clear the loan and get the NOC *before* looking for buyers.</p>
</div>
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 3: Hiding Previous Accident History</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm mt-2"><strong>What Happens:</strong> Professional inspectors (like ours) will find it, and your price will crash during negotiation.</p>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm"><strong>Fix:</strong> Be transparent. A car with a well-repaired dent is better than one with hidden chassis damage.</p>
</div>
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 4: Not Informing the Insurance Company</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm"><strong>Fix:</strong> Once the RC is transferred, you must officially notify your insurer to avoid 'Double Insurance' issues.</p>
</div>
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 5: Relying on Generic Online Prices</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm"><strong>Fix:</strong> Raipur market demand is unique. Local demand for SUVs is much higher than in other cities. Get a local appraisal.</p>
