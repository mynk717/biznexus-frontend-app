  <h2 id="gmb">2. Mastering the Google Business Profile (GBP)</h2>
  <p>The "Map Pack" is the top 3 results on Google Maps. To dominate this space in Raipur, you need a technically perfect profile:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>NAP Consistency:</strong> Your Name, Address, and Phone number must be identical on your website, GMB, and every local directory. Even a small difference like "Rd" vs "Road" can confuse Google's crawler.</li>
    <li><strong>Local Citations:</strong> Don't just list your business on national sites. You need to be listed in <strong>Chhattisgarh-specific directories</strong> and local Raipur business indexes.</li>
    <li><strong>Verified Reviews:</strong> Reviews from local IPs (Raipur-based) carry significantly more weight than reviews from outside the city.</li>
  </ul>
