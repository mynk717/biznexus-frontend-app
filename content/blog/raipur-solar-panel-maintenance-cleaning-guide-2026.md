<h2 id="diy-mistakes">4. 3 Common DIY Cleaning Mistakes to Avoid</h2>
<div class="space-y-4 my-8">
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 1: Cleaning under the midday sun</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm">Cold water on 70°C glass can cause **Micro-cracks**. Only clean before 8 AM or after 6 PM.</p>
</div>
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 2: Using hard Raipur tap water</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm">High TDS water leaves salt deposits (scaling). Use soft water or specialized solar cleaners.</p>
</div>
<div class="bg-slate-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-yellow-500">
<h3 class="font-bold text-slate-900 mb-2">❌ Mistake 3: Pressure washing directly</h3>
<p class="text-slate-700 text-sm">High pressure can damage the panel seals. Use a low-pressure mop or sponge.</p>
</div>
