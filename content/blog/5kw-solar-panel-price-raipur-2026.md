  <h2 id="usage">2. Is a 5kW System Right for You?</h2>
  <p>A 5kW system generates approximately **22 to 25 units per day**. In Raipur, this is enough to power:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>3 to 4 Inverter ACs (1.5 Ton) during the day.</li>
    <li>Complete home lighting, fans, and high-load appliances (Geyser/Washing Machine).</li>
    <li>Ideal for families with monthly CSPDCL bills between **₹4,500 to ₹7,500**.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 id="bottlenecks">3. The 5kW Challenge: Structural Safety & Vendor Delays</h2>
  <p>As we discussed in our <a href="/blog/1kw-solar-panel-price-raipur-2026" class="text-blue-600 underline">1kW guide</a>, vendor distraction is a major issue. However, for 5kW systems, the **structural audit** is the most frequent delay point.</p>
  <p>A 5kW array typically requires **10 to 12 large panels**, adding significant weight to your rooftop. In Raipur, CSPDCL engineers are more rigorous during the Technical Feasibility (TF) stage for 5kW+ systems. Many vendors fail to provide the necessary "Structure Certificate," which leads to your subsidy application being rejected or delayed for months.</p>

  <h2 id="roi">4. The ROI Math: Why 5kW Beats 3kW for Large Homes</h2>
  <p>While the upfront cost is higher, the **cost-per-watt** for a 5kW system is much lower than a 3kW or 1kW system. Because the labor and inverter synchronization costs are similar, you get "Bulk Pricing" benefits. Most Raipur bungalows see a full return on investment (ROI) within **38 to 42 months**.</p>
